- [Visual Timer by Yong](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1458639178) offers a clear and easy-to-understand way to keep track of time through visual cues. It includes flexible settings that let you tailor timers to different situations and personal needs. A thoughtful tool for anyone who prefers a more visual approach to managing time.

- [Vanilla](https://note.edykim.com/) is a simple agenda-style digital planner, available as a free PDF download.
